About CPTED Canada

Who They Are

CPTED Canada is a non-profit education and networking organization focused on Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design. It grew from CPTED Ontario, founded in 2001.

Mission

Their mission is to reduce both fear of crime and crime itself by advancing CPTED practices, with the goal of improving quality of life across communities.

What They Do

They promote safer community design through advocacy, education, and professional networking.

How They Do It

They share materials, blogs, website resources, hands-on training, conferences, and regional meetings that teach practical CPTED principles.

Source: CPTED Canada About Us and Code of Conduct.
